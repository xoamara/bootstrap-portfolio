About Me

Hello, my name is Neri Stec. I am a Full Stack Web Developer based in the Seattle area with a background in aerospace, supply chain, and program management. I want to bring my passion to effectively integrate between business to the world of coding.

In my professional career I have always been determined to find efficient ways of improving the my work flow and the work flow of the customer. As a web developer I have the opportunity to create applications to do just that. I am driven to understand the needs of the user in order to translate that to a build that not only meet the customer's needs but maximize the user's experience.

Outside work I am an avid gamer with a love for RPGs and puzzles. I am always up for a challenge in and out of the workplace!
